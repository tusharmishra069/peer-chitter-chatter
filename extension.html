About Our Extension

Our extension simplifies the process of establishing direct connections. Here's how it works:

  • Open the extension popup menu.
  • Enter the code provided in the popup menu.
  • Once the code is entered, a direct connection is initiated seamlessly.

This makes it incredibly easy to connect and communicate without any hassle. Whether you're sharing files, chatting, or video calling, our extension ensures a smooth and efficient experience.
